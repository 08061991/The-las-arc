In this episode, Dino leads the discussion into one of the biggest mysteries of the 80s/early 90s, the La's. Massively influential to every single record released the next 15 years- Lee Mavers' perfectionism saw to it for him to tell his fans "Don't buy the record."  
  
There was probably more money spent recording his first record than any debut in the last 20 years, yet it only saw the light of day when the record company forced their hand. Dino and Tony trace the history following this, and Dino shares a precious story about their promotional tour (uncomfortable setting) in which Dino encountered the band.
